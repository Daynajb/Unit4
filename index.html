PIGEON PREFERS THE SUBWAY

This pigeon sure knew the better way to travel in chilly February weather! The funny thing was that everyone ignored it just like it was a regular human train rider... and it so calmly left the train when its stop came! The doors only opened for a few seconds, and yet it walked out confidently in perfect timing.
